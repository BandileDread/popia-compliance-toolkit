## 2. ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES

As Information Officer, your duties include:

### 2.1 Encouraging Compliance
- Ensure the organization complies with POPIA
- Develop and implement data protection policies
- Monitor compliance across all departments

### 2.2 Dealing with Requests
- Handle data subject access requests (requests to view personal information)
- Respond to queries about data processing
- Coordinate with the Information Regulator when required

### 2.3 Working with the Regulator
- Liaise with the Information Regulator on compliance matters
- Report data breaches as required by Section 22
- Cooperate with investigations

### 2.4 Staff Awareness
- Ensure staff are aware of POPIA requirements
- Provide or arrange training on data protection
- Communicate policy updates

### 2.5 Additional Responsibilities
- Maintain the data processing register
- Review and update privacy policies
- Conduct data protection impact assessments when required
- Ensure appropriate security measures are in place

---

## 3. AUTHORITY

You are authorized to:
- Access all records and systems containing personal information
- Request reports and information from all departments
- Make decisions regarding data protection policies
- Represent the organization in data protection matters
- Delegate duties to Deputy Information Officers as needed

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

This appointment:
- Continues until you resign or are replaced
- Must be renewed if you change roles within the organization
- Automatically terminates if you leave the organization

Upon termination, you must:
- Hand over all data protection records
- Brief your successor
- Ensure continuity of compliance
